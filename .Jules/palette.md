# Palette's Journal - Kojo Kwarteng Photography Portfolio

## 2025-05-14 - Initial Assessment
**Learning:** High-fashion photography portfolios often prioritize visual aesthetics over accessibility, leading to missing keyboard navigation and focus management in custom grid engines and lightboxes.
**Action:** Implement keyboard support (Enter/Space to open, Arrows to navigate, Escape to close) and dynamic `tabindex` management for the "Platinum Grid Engine" to ensure accessibility without compromising the luxury feel.
